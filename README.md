# 🚀 Full Stack Interview Preparation (3–5+ Years)

Welcome to the **Full Stack Interview Preparation** repository!

This repository is a collection of **real interview questions and detailed answers** for **3–5+ years experienced Full Stack Developers**. It covers topics that are frequently asked in **product-based companies**, **mid-sized companies**, and **high-growth startups**.

## 🎯 Why We Created This Repository

This year, I set a personal goal to attend as many interviews as possible—not just to find a better opportunity, but also to overcome interview fear and improve my problem-solving and communication skills.

While preparing, I realized that many interview questions are repeated across different companies. Instead of keeping my notes private, my friends and I decided to build this repository so that anyone preparing for Full Stack interviews can benefit from our learning.

Our goal is to create a practical interview guide that focuses on **real-world questions**, **clear explanations**, and **interview-ready answers**.
